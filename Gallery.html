The Glimps of Real Indian Heroes

Discription:

  • Subhash Chandra Bose was a prominent leader who believed in armed struggle against British rule.
  • He formed the Azad Hind Fauj (Indian National Army) to fight the British with the support of Japan during World War II.
  • His famous slogan “Give me blood, and I will give you freedom” inspired countless Indians to join the freedom movement.
  • Bose strongly opposed the non-violent approach and parted ways with the Indian National Congress leadership.
  • He is remembered as “Netaji”, a title given to him for his fearless leadership and dedication to India's independence.
